# Ranged Weapons Utility Analysis — Elden Ring (Base + DLC)

**Purpose:** Bows, crossbows, greatbows, ballistas, and their ammunition serve a utilitarian function in Elden Ring — pulling, puzzle-solving, status application, openers, and situational damage. This analysis evaluates all ranged weapons and ammo types through that lens, not as primary damage dealers.

---

For the companion tool specifically: crossbows are the ideal recommendation when the walkthrough requires "shoot something" (fire pillars, distant switches) and the player has no bow equipped. A Light Crossbow is purchasable for 1,500 runes at Weeping Peninsula with only 12 STR / 8 DEX — virtually every build qualifies.

## Arrow Damage Scaling — Critical Mechanics

**This is widely misunderstood and matters for the companion tool:**

1. **Arrow elemental damage scales with the bow's stat scaling, not the arrow's.** Fire arrow damage scales with the bow's FTH scaling (if any). Lightning arrow damage scales with the bow's DEX scaling. Magic arrow damage scales with the bow's INT scaling.

2. **Split damage on arrows suffers double defense penalty.** A Fire Arrow (10 Phys + 90 Fire) goes through both physical AND fire defense, losing more total damage than a 100 Phys arrow against most targets. This means **standard physical arrows are usually the best DPS option** for bows not built around elemental scaling.

3. **Status buildup is independent of damage.** Bleed, Poison, Sleep, and Frost arrows apply their buildup regardless of how much damage the hit deals. This makes status arrows valuable even on unupgraded bows.

4. **Serpent Bow adds +15 Poison to ALL arrows** including Bloodbone (creating dual Poison+Bleed application). Its ARC scaling enhances Poison, Bleed, and Sleep buildup on status arrows.

5. **You can only carry 99 of each arrow type.** Two active arrow slots. This limits sustained ranged combat and makes arrow choice per engagement critical.

## Utility Recommendations by Situation

### "I need to shoot something and I don't have a ranged weapon"

**Cheapest solution:** Light Crossbow — 1,500 runes from Weeping Peninsula merchant. Requires only 12 STR / 8 DEX (every class qualifies except pure caster edge cases). Can fire one-handed from offhand. Buy regular Bolts (30r each) from any merchant.

**Better solution:** Longbow — 1,200 runes from Twin Maiden Husks at Roundtable Hold. Requires 9 STR / 14 DEX. Better range and accepts AoW.

### Stormfoot Catacombs fire pillar puzzle (and similar fire-trigger puzzles)

**Requirement:** Fire Arrows. Purchasable from merchants (120r each). Any bow works. Fire Arrows ignite oil pools and activate flame pillars. The companion tool should flag this requirement before the player enters the dungeon.

**Minimum loadout:** Any bow + 10 Fire Arrows. Total cost: ~2,400 runes (Longbow 1,200 + 10 Fire Arrows 1,200).

### Pulling / aggro control

Any ranged weapon works. **Light Crossbow in offhand** is ideal — pre-load a bolt, fire instantly without sheathing your melee weapon, immediately resume melee.

### Status application at range

**Sleep:** Pulley Crossbow + Sleepbone Bolts = 234 sleep buildup per trigger pull (3 × 78). Best ranged sleep delivery in the game. Devastating against Godskin Duo and other sleep-susceptible bosses.

**Bleed:** Serpent Bow + Bloodbone Arrows (Fletched) = 85 bleed + 15 poison per arrow. Dual status application. Or Pulley Crossbow + Bloodbone Bolts = 195 bleed per trigger pull.

**Frost:** Coldbone Arrows have 100 frost buildup per arrow. Frost proc = target takes 20% increased damage for 30 seconds. Excellent debuff opener before engaging melee.

**Scarlet Rot:** Rotbone Arrows apply Rot at range. Against rot-susceptible bosses, 2-3 applications can proc Rot (5% HP per second). Fire and forget — switch to melee while Rot ticks.

### Sniper / long-range engagement

**Pulley Bow** has the longest range of any standard bow (55). Combined with Arrow's Reach Talisman and Mighty Shot AoW, it enables extreme-range kills. Useful for pulling specific enemies from groups and for long-distance puzzle solutions.

**Jar Cannon** delivers 672 base damage per shot — no scaling needed. With Explosive Greatbolts (150 Fire), total output exceeds 800 per shot. Use as opener before boss engagements for free damage.

### Boss fight opener (free damage before aggro)

1. **Jar Cannon + Explosive Greatbolt** = ~820 damage, zero stat investment beyond 23 effective STR (2H)
2. **Golem Greatbow + Radahn's Spear** from stealth = 2,000+ with Through and Through
3. **Coldbone Arrow** to apply Frost debuff (20% increased damage) before engaging melee — mathematically the highest-value opener for melee builds

## Companion Tool Relevance

### What the walkthrough engine needs to know:

1. **Fire Arrows are a progression gate for Stormfoot Catacombs** — the companion should flag this before the player enters. Ensure the player has a bow + Fire Arrows or knows where to acquire them.

2. **Any ranged weapon satisfies "shoot the thing" requirements.** The cheapest path is Light Crossbow (1,500r, 12 STR / 8 DEX) + Bolts (30r each). This should be the default recommendation when a walkthrough step requires ranged capability.

3. **Crafting Kit unlocks Bone Arrows/Bolts** — infinite (if tedious) ammo supply. This reinforces DD36 (Crafting Kit mandatory for all classes).

4. **Specific boss tactical needs** that involve ranged weapons:
   - Sleep delivery against Godskin encounters → Pulley Crossbow + Sleepbone Bolts
   - Rot application against rot-susceptible bosses → Rotbone Arrows from any bow
   - Frost opener for damage amplification → Coldbone Arrows

5. **Status arrows don't require bow investment.** An unupgraded Shortbow firing Sleepbone Arrows applies the same 78 sleep buildup as a +25 Albinauric Bow. Status utility is independent of bow upgrade level.

6. **Weight budget matters.** A Light Crossbow (3.5 weight) in the offhand equipment slot is the most weight-efficient ranged utility option. For builds already carrying heavy melee weapons, this matters.
